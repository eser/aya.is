# Privacy Nutrition Label — Questionnaire Answers

## Overview

AYA is a read-only content browser. It does not collect, store, or share any user data. There is no login, no analytics, no advertising SDK, and no tracking of any kind.

## Data Collection Declaration

**Do you or your third-party partners collect data from this app?**
→ **No**

## Tracking Declaration

**Do you or your third-party partners use data from this app to track users?**
→ **No**

The app does not participate in cross-app or cross-site tracking.

## Technical Details

- Network requests go only to `api.aya.is` to fetch public content (stories, events, profiles).
- `URLCache` is used for standard HTTP response caching on-device. No data is sent to external analytics or advertising services.
- No third-party SDKs are included.
- No device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV) are accessed.
